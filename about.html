About Dough House

The perfect bakery for you and your pets

Nestled in the heart of Manila, Dough House offers a charming retreat for pastry lovers and their Furbabies. With its cozy decor and the aroma of freshly baked bread, it invites passersby into a world of rustic charm.
Our bakery prides itself on handcrafted pastries and artisanal bread, made using locally sourced, organic ingredients. From our famous Tiramisu to our new Blueberry Pie, each item tells a story of tradition and passion.

Mission

Our purpose is to bake fresh, authentic, and scrumptious bread, while promoting healthy eating to bring truly wholesome, and irresistibly delicious artisanal baked goods for you and your pets every day.

Vision

We dream of being recognized as an environmentally-conscious bakery, leading the industry in sustainable procedures, from farm to oven to retail.
